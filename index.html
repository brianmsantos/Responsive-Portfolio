About Me

Brian Santos was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. Brian's father was in the Navy and his mother was a nurse. Due to the nature of his father being in the military they moved around a lot.

Brian has lived in San Diego, Chula Vista to be specific most of his life. Brian's hobbies include Crossfit, weightlifting, hiking, and playing with his two Siberian Huskies.
